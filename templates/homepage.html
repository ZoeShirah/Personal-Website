Bio

Zoe graduated from University of California Santa Cruz with a Bachelor's Degree in Astrophysics. She got her first taste of software engineering with an Intro to C class she loved. After graduation, Zoe worked in an Astrophysics research lab where she enjoyed work on the command line and digging into documentation. She then transitioned to working at a medical office where she greatly improved her interpersonal skills, all the while continuing her love of programming by taking 3 Java classes at the local community college. The more classes she took, the more she realized her passion for the logic and problem solving of engineering.
